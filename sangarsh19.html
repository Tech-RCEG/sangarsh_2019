Sangarsh'19

Sangarsh with a tagline of "transforming lives" has been running untiringly for over 16 years and has now stepped into its 17 edition.Sangarsh'19 has parked it's mission vechile for the intellectually and differently abled people of "Pari Paul Mentally Disabled Home" located at Sankarankovil town, Tirunelveli district.These differently abled people have difficulties to fulfill their daily needs and we have aspired to lend our shoulders by aiding them with proper physiotherapy equipments and a friendly environment which would make them overthrow their hardships and to transform their dreams into tomorrow's reality.
